About Us

Marketing is a fundamental part of business management and commerce that involves planning and executing activities to satisfy and retain customers. Its main goal is to create value for customers by attracting new ones and keeping existing ones. Marketing can be conducted by sellers, such as manufacturers or retailers, and can be directed at other businesses or consumers.

    Marketing activities include:
  • Identifying customer needs
  • Developing products and services
  • Communicating product information
